East Indian Places !

East India is rich in culture and natural beauty. Darjeeling in West Bengal offers scenic views and tea gardens. Odisha is known for its temples and the beach city Puri. In the northeast, Sikkim and Shillong are full of green hills, clean air, and peaceful lakes. These places are perfect for those who love calm and nature.
