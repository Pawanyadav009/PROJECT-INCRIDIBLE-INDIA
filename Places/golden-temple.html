Golden Temple - Jewel of Faith

The Golden Temple, or Harmandir Sahib, located in Amritsar, Punjab, is the most sacred site of Sikhism. Originally built in the 16th century by Guru Arjan Dev, it is covered in gold and surrounded by the holy Amrit Sarovar. The temple stands for equality and devotion, welcoming visitors of all faiths. The Langar (community kitchen) serves free meals daily to thousands of devotees.
