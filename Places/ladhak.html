Ladakh - Land of High Passes

Ladakh, known as the "Land of High Passes," is one of the most breathtaking destinations in India. Surrounded by the Himalayas and Karakoram ranges, it offers rugged landscapes, monasteries, and unique Tibetan culture.
Famous spots like Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, and Magnetic Hill make it a dream destination for adventurers, bikers, and peace seekers alike.
