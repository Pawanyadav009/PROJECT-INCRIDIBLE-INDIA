Kerala Backwaters - God's Own Country

Kerala Backwaters are a network of serene lagoons, lakes, and canals running parallel to the Arabian Sea. Known for lush green coconut trees, houseboat rides, and peaceful villages, this destination gives a glimpse of traditional Kerala life.
Alleppey, Kumarakom, and Kollam are the most famous backwater regions where tourists enjoy houseboat cruises, birdwatching, and authentic Kerala cuisine.
